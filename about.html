About HERR

Healthy Experiences, Rest, and Recovery

HERR is a wellness movement for women focused on fitness, mindfulness, and self-care. We host intimate gatherings that bring together amazing vendors and health-conscious women.

Who We Are

We combine fitness, mindfulness, and community to help you pause, restore, and take care of yourself. Our events create a safe space where women can feel empowered, grounded, and whole.

Our Mission

To inspire well-being through rest, restoration, and empowerment. We believe true health comes from nurturing both body and mind, and every woman deserves to prioritize herself.
